Coding for Map Screen

Map Screen

Now, we will be creating logic to find the location of the device on which this app is installed.

Logic

  1. Click on the "Screen2".
  2. Click on the "Button1" and drag the "when Button1 Click" block.
  3. Go to the "Control" and drag the "navigate to Screen1" block.
  4. Click on the "Map1" and drag the "when Map1 onMapReady" block.
  5. Click on the "Location_Sensor1" and drag the "call Location_Sensor1's GetCurrentLocation" block.
  6. Click on the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Latitude" block.
  7. Drag the "Latitude" block and place it at "37.78".
  8. Click on the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Longitude" block.
  9. Drag the "Longitude" block and place it at "-122.4".
  10. Click on the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Zoom" block.
  11. Change the value of "set Map1's Zoom" from "0" to "5".
  12. Click on the "Map1" and drag the "call Map1's addMaker" block.
  13. Write "Hey!" next to title and "We Find You" next to description.
  14. Final screen.
