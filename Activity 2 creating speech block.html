Quiz Screen Logic

Now, we will make this app speak with the help of the "say" block.

Logic

  1. Click on the "Screen 2".
  2. Go to the "Image1" and drag the "when Image1 Click" block.
  3. Go to the "Speech" block and drag the "Say "hello" in English(en-US)" block.
  4. Change the text "Hello" to "There is one bus on the road" in the "say" block.
  5. Click on the "Screen 3".
  6. Go to the "Image2" and drag the "when Image2 Click" block.
  7. Go to the "Speech" block and drag the "Say "hello" in English(en-US)" block.
  8. Change "Hello" from the block to "There is one bus on the road".
  9. Click on the "Screen 4".
