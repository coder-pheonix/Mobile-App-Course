Coding for horizontal movement of "Robot"

Game Screen

Now, we will make a "Robot" move horizontally.

Logic

  1. Go to the "Right" and drag a "when Right Click" block.
  2. Go to the "Variables" and drag "set app variable x to" block.
  3. Go to the "Math" and drag the second block.
  4. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable x" block.
  5. Change the text "1" to "10".
  6. Go to the "Motion" block and drag a "move Sprite8 to x 100 y 100" block.
  7. Change the text "Sprite8" to "Sprite1".
  8. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable x" block.
  9. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable y" block.
  10. Go to the "Left" and drag a "when Left Click" block.
  11. Go to the "Variables" and drag "set app variable x to" block.
  12. Go to the "Math" and drag the second block.
  13. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable x" block.
  14. Change the text "1" to "10".
  15. Go to the "Motion" block and drag a "move Sprite8 to x 100 y 100" block.
  16. Change the text "Sprite8" to "Sprite1".
  17. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable x" block.
  18. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable y" block.
