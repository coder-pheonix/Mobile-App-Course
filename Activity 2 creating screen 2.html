Creating the UI of screen 2 of the Image Quiz app

  1. Now, we will create Screen 2 for the Image Quiz app.
  2. Screen 2 consists of two components "Button" and "Image".
  3. In Screen 2, there is a question with two options.

The steps to make Screen 2 are as follows.

  1. Click on the "+" to create a new screen.
  2. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Button" on the screen.
  3. Change the name of the "Button" to "Score1".
  4. Change the text of "Button" to "Score".
  5. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Image" on the screen.
  6. Change the coordinates, height and width of the "Image".
  7. Download the image from the Link and upload it from your system.
  8. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Button" on the screen.
  9. Change the coordinates, height and width of the "Button".
  10. Change the name of the "Button".
  11. Change the text of the "Button".
  12. Go to the "Add Components" and drag another "Button" on the screen.
  13. Change the name of the "Button".
  14. Change the text of the "Button".
  15. Change the coordinates, height and width of the "Button".
  16. Final Screen.
