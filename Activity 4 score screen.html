Creating UI of score screen

Score Screen

Now, we will make screen 3 that displays score.

Screen 3 is consists of two components "Label" and "Button".

The steps to create screen 3 are as follows.

  1. Click on the "+".
  2. Change the name from "Label" to "Your Score".
  3. Change text "Your Score" of label to "bold".
  4. Align the text "Your Score" of label to "center".
  5. Drag a "Button" to the screen.
  6. Change the "Text" of button to "0".
  7. Change the "Text Color" to "Yellow".
  8. Change "Essentials" from "18" to "45".
  9. Change the "Height" to "120" and "Width" to "70" of button.
  10. Change the "Background" color "blue" to "black".
  11. Make the text "0" of the button to bold.
  12. Drag a "Button" to the screen.
  13. Change the name of button to "Play Again".
  14. Change the text "Button" to "Play Again".
  15. Change the "Text Color" to "Yellow".
  16. Change the "Text Color" to "Yellow".
  17. Change the values of "X" to "108" and "Y" to "452". Change height to "38" and width to "160".
  18. Final Screen.
