"Map Screen" and "Introductory Screen"

Now, we will create UI for the Map screen and logic for the Introductory screen.

The steps to create UI for screen 2 and logic for screen 1 are as follows.

  1. Click on the "+" and create a new screen.
  2. Go to the "Background" and change the "Background" colour of the screen to "Purple".
  3. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Button" on the screen.
  4. Change the text of the "Button" to "Home".
  5. Change the font of the text of "Button" to "Montserrat".
  6. Change the Text Color of the "Button" to "black".
  7. Go to the "Essentials" and change the text to "Bold" and "Italic".
  8. Go to the "Essentials" and change the text size from "18" to "24".
  9. Go to the "Layout", Change the value of X to "25" and Y to "600". Change height to "40" and width to "325".
  10. Go to the "Background" and change the background colour of the "Button" to white.
  11. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Map" on the screen.
  12. Go to the "Advanced Properties" and click the slider of "User Location", "My Location", and "Traffic".
  13. Go to the "Layout", Change the value of X to "0" and Y to "10". Change height to "575" and width to "375".
  14. Click on the "Blocks".
  15. Go to the "Screen1" and drag the "when Screen1 Opens".
  16. Go to the "Control" and drag the "wait 1 seconds" block.
  17. Change "wait 1 seconds" to "wait 3 seconds".
  18. Go to the "Control" and drag the "navigate to Screen1" block.
  19. Change the "Screen1" to "Screen2".
