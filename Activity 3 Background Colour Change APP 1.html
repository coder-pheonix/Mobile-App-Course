Creating the "first app(Background Color Changer App)"

Design

  1. This app consists of two "Buttons".
  2. On clicking the red "Button", the background changes to red.
  3. And, on clicking the blue "Button", the background changes to blue.

The steps to create the app are as follows.

  1. Drag the "Button" and place it on the screen.
  2. Change the name of the "Button" to "Red".
  3. Change the text of the "Button" to "Red".
  4. Change the "Background" color of the "Button" to red.
  5. Drag the "Button" and place it on the screen.
  6. Change the name of the "Button" to "Blue".
  7. Change the text of the "Button" to "Blue".
  8. Click on the "Blocks".
  9. Go to the "Blue" and drag the "When Blue Click" block.
  10. Go to the "Screen1" and drag the "set Screen1's Background Color" block.
  11. Change the "Background Color" to blue.
  12. Go to the "Red" and drag the "When Red Click" block.
  13. Go to the "Screen1" and drag the "set Screen1's Background Color" block.
  14. Change the "Background Color" to red block.
