Creating UI for "Geo Location Sensor App"

In this lecture, we will be creating UI of "Geo Location Sensor App".

  1. This app will display the location of the device.
  2. In this app there is only one screen.
  3. Screen 1 is consist of a "Button", a "Label" and an "Animation".

The steps to create UI are as follows.

  1. Click on the "Create New App".
  2. Click on the "New Project Name:" and type "Geo Location Sensor"
  3. Click on the "Category:" and select "Navigation" from the drop down list.
  4. Click on the "Create" button.
  5. Go to the "Add Components" and drag a "Button" to the screen.
  6. Change the name of the "Button" to "Get My Location".
  7. Change the text of the "Button" to "Get My Location".
  8. Change the "Text Color" of the text of "Button".
  9. Change the text of the "Button" to bold.
  10. Go to the "Essentials" and change the text size from "18" to "25".
  11. Go to the "Layout", Change the value of X to "70" and Y to "50". Change the height to "50" and width to "250".
  12. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label" on the screen.
  13. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Animation" on the screen.
  14. Go to the "Layout", Change the value of X to "105" and Y to "375". Change the height to "170" and width to "170".
  15. Click on the "Screen1", download image from link upload image as the background image of the screen.
  16. Copy "Lottie Animation URL" and Paste it at the source.
