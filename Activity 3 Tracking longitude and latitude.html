Coding for setting latitude and longitude based on location

Logic Screen

In this session, we will create logic to find the latitude and longitude.

Logic

  1. Click on the "Label1" and drag the "set Label1's text to Label" block.
  2. Go to the "Text" block and drag the "join" block.
  3. Change "hello" to "Latitude is " block.
  4. Go to the "Text" block and drag the "join" block.
  5. Drag the "Latitude" block and place it at the "hello" block.
  6. Delete the "hello" block.
  7. Go to the "Text" block and drag the "join" block.
  8. Delete the "world" block.
  9. Change the text "hello" into "And Longitude is" block.
  10. Drag the "Longitude" block and place it at the "world" block.
  11. Delete the "world" block.
