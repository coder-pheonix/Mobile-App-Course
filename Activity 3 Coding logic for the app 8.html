Coding for Screen 2

Screen 2 of Weather Forecasting

  1. In splash screen 2, add List_Viewer1.
  2. Add the name of three cities and the longitude & latitude of these cities.

The steps to code the remaining "Screen 2" are as follows.

  1. Go to the "List_Viewer1" and drag the "when List_Viewer1 Item Click" block.
  2. Go to the "List_Viewer1" and drag the "set List_Viewer1's Height to" block.
  3. Go to the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Height to" block.
  4. Change the value from "0" to "300".
  5. Go to the "TempratureLabel" and drag the "set TempratureLabel's Height to" block.
  6. Change the value from "0" to "100".
  7. Go to the "SelectCityButton" and drag the "set SelectCityButton's Text to" block.
  8. Drag the "item" block and place it at "Button".
  9. Go to the "Control" and drag the "if do" block.
  10. Go to the "Logic" and drag the first block.
  11. Go to the "SelectCityButton" and drag the "SelectCityButton's Text" block.
  12. Go to the "Text" and drag the first block.
  13. Write "San Francisco" in the block.
  14. Go to the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Latitude to" block.
  15. Change the value from "37.78" to "37.7749".
  16. Go to the "Map1" and drag the "set Map1's Longitude to" block. Also, change the value from "-122.4" to "122.4194".
  17. Click on the blue icon and place "else if" below "if".
  18. Click on the blue icon to minimize it.
  19. Similarly, add the longitude, Latitude of London and New York.
