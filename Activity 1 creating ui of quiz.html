Creating UI for image quiz app

In this lecture, we will create the UI of an image quiz game.

  1. This app consists of four screens.
  2. Screen 1 is the introduction screen.
  3. Screen 2 and Screen 3 are quiz screens.
  4. Screen 4 is a score screen, and it displays the score.

Creating splash screen UI of the image quiz game.

  1. In this screen, we use two components, "Label" and "Button".
  2. "Screen 1" navigates to "Screen 2" after 3 seconds.

The steps to make the first screen are as follows.

  1. Click on the "Start Building".
  2. Click on the "New Project Name:" and type "Image Quiz App" and also click on the "Category:" and select "Education" from the option.
  3. Click on the "Create" button.
  4. Change the "Background" colour.
  5. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label" to the screen.
  6. Change the name of the "Label" to Title.
  7. Change the text of the "Label" to Quiz.
  8. Change the "Font" of the text of the "Label".
  9. Change the text of the "Label" to bold.
  10. Center align the text of the "Label".
  11. Change the text colour of the "Label" to white. Go to "Add Components" and add another "Label" and game instructions as the text of "Label".
  12. Go to the "Add Components" and search "Animation" in the search bar. Drag it on the screen.
  13. Now open the website "LottieFiles" and click "Sign Up".
  14. Search "Quiz" in the search bar.
  15. Copy the "Lottie Animation URL".
  16. Paste the "Lottie Animation URL" at the source.
  17. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Button" on the screen.
  18. Change the "Button" name to "start".
  19. Change the "Button" text to "Let's Play".
  20. Change the "Background" colour of the "Button" to "Black".
  21. Final Screen.
