Weather Forecast Screen

Now, we will create UI for the "Weather Forecast Screen".

The steps to create UI are as follows.

  1. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Button".
  2. Change the "Button1" to "SelectCityButton".
  3. Change the text "Button" to "San Francisco".
  4. Click on the "Text Color" to "Blue".
  5. Go to the "Essential" and change the "font-size" from "18" to "24".
  6. Go to the "Layout" and change the value of "X" to "90" and "Y" to "50". Also, change the "height" to "50" and "width" to "200."
  7. Go to the "Style" and change the "Background" colour to "white".
  8. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "List Viewer".
  9. Go to the "Layout" and change the value of the "X" to "90" and "Y" to "150". Also, change the "height" to "150" and "width" to "200."
  10. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label".
  11. Change the "Label1" to "TempratureLabel".
  12. Delete the text "Label1".
  13. Change the "Font size" from "18" to "60".
  14. Go to the "Layout" and change the value of the "X" to "100" and "Y" to "200". Also change the "height" to "70" and "width" to "180."
  15. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label".
  16. Change the text "Label1" to "GiveCreditLabel".
  17. Change the text "Label" to "powered by the Open Weather Map".
  18. Change the "Font size" from "18" to "14".
  19. Go to the "Layout" and change the value of the "X" to "100" and "Y" to "300". Also, change the "height" to "70" and "width" to "200".
  20. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Map".
