Creating UI for the "Geo Location with Map Integration" app

This app consists of two screens. The first is the "Introductory Screen", and the second is the "Map Screen".

  1. The Introductory Screen consists of two components "Label" and "Animation".
  2. The Map Screen consists of two components "Map" and "Button".

The steps to create the UI of the app are as follows.

  1. Click on the "Create New App".
  2. Click on the "New Project Name:" and type "Geo Location with Map Integration".
  3. Click on the "Category:" and select "Navigation" from the option.
  4. Click on the "Create" button.
  5. Go to the "Background" and change the "Background" colour of the screen to "Purple".
  6. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label" on the screen.
  7. Change the text of the "Label" to "BeSingular".
  8. Change the "Text Color" of "Label" to white.
  9. Center align the text of the "Label".
  10. Go to the "Essentials" and change the text size from "18" to "24".
  11. Go to the "Layout", change the value of X to "70" and Y to "135". Change the height to "40" and the width to "250".
  12. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label" on the screen.
  13. Change the text of the "Label" to "Location Finder".
  14. Change the "Text Color" of "Label" to white.
  15. Go to the "Essentials" and change the text size from "18" to "30".
  16. Go to the "Layout", change the value of X to "70" and Y to "225". Change the height to "50" and the width to "250".
  17. Center align the text of the "Label".
  18. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Animation" on the screen.
  19. Go to the "Layout", change the value of X to "70" and Y to "310". Change the height to "225" and the width to "250".
  20. Copy "Lottie Animation URL" and paste it at the source.
