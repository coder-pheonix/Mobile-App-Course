Splash Screen Logic

In this lecture, we create logic to make the app navigate from "screen1" to "Screen2" after 3 seconds and also create logic for the "Buttons".

Logic

  1. Click on the "Blocks" tab.
  2. Click on the "Start" and drag the "when start click" block.
  3. Go to the "Control" and drag the "navigate to Screen 1" block.
  4. Click on the "Screen 1" and select "Screen 2" from the drop-down.
  5. Go to the "Control" and drag the "wait 1 seconds" block.
  6. Change the "wait 1 seconds" to "wait 3 seconds".
  7. Click on the "Screen 2".
  8. Click on the "one" and drag the "when one click" block.
  9. Click on the "one" and drag the "set one's Background Color to" block.
  10. Change the background color to "green".
  11. Go to the "Control" and drag the "wait 1 seconds" block.
  12. Change the "wait 1 seconds" to "wait 3 seconds".
  13. Go to the "Control" and drag the "navigate to Screen 1" block.
  14. Change the "navigate to Screen 1" to "navigate to Screen 2".
  15. Click on the "two" and drag the "when two click" block.
  16. Click on the "two" and drag the "set two's Background Color to" block.
  17. Change the background color to "red".
  18. Click on the "two" and drag the "set two's Background Color to" block.
  19. Change the background color to "green".

  21. Change the "wait 1 seconds" to "wait 3 seconds".
  22. Go to the "Control" and drag the "navigate to Screen 1" block.
  23. Change the "navigate to Screen 1" to "navigate to Screen 2".
  24. Click on the "Screen 3".
  25. Click on the "three" and drag the "when three Click" block.
