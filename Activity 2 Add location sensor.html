Adding Location Sensor

Now, we are going to code logic for the splash screen.

Logic

  1. Click on the "Blocks".
  2. Click on the "Get My Location" and drag the "when Get My Location Click" block to the screen.
  3. Click on the "Sensors" and select "Location Sensor" from the four sensors.
  4. Click on the "Submit".
  5. Go to the "Location_Sensor1" and drag the "call Location_Sensor1's GetCurrentLocation" block.
