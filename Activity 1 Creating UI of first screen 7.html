Splash Screen UI

In this lecture, we will create UI for Weather Forecasting App.

This app consists of two screens. The first is the introductory screen, and second is weather forecasting screen.

The steps to create UI for screen 1 are as follows.

  1. Click on the "Create New App".
  2. Click on the "New Project Name:" and type "Weather Forecasting App".
  3. Click on the "Category:" and select "Weather" from the drop-down.
  4. Click on the "Create" button.
  5. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Label".
  6. Change the text "Label" to "Weather Forecasting App".
  7. Click on the "B" to "Bold" the text and Font size from "18" to "25".
  8. Go to the "Layout". Change the value of "X" to "40" and "Y" to "40". Also, change the "height" to "40" and "width" to "300".
  9. Go to the "Add Components" and drag the "Animation".
  10. Click on the Link, copy "Lottie Animation URL", and paste it.
  11. Go to the "Layout". Change the value of "X" to "100" and "Y" to "250". Also, change the "height" to "200" and "width" to "200".
  12. Change the "Background" colour to "purple".
  13. Final Screen.
