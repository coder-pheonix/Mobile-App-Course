Coding logic for "Weather Forecasting App"

In this session, we will be coding logic for the "Weather Forecasting App".

  1. This app consists of two screens.
  2. Screen 1 is the introduction screen.
  3. Screen 2 is a weather forecasting screen.

Coding splash screen logic of the Weather Forecasting App.

  1. In this screen, we use two components, "Label" and "Animation".
  2. Screen 1 opens for 3 seconds, and then "Screen 1" navigates to "Screen 2".

The steps to code for the first screen are as follows.

  1. Click on the "Blocks" tab.
  2. Click on the "When Screen1 Opens" and drag it on the screen.
  3. Go to the "Control" block and drag the "Wait 1 seconds" block.
  4. Change the "Wait 1 seconds" to "Wait 3 seconds".
  5. Go to the "Control" block and drag the "navigate to Screen1" block.
  6. Change the "Screen1" to "Screen2".
  7. Click on the "Screen2".
  8. Go to the "Variables" block and drag the "initialize app variable name to" block.
  9. Change the "name" to "APIKey".
  10. Go to the "Variables" block and drag the "initialize app variable name to" block. Also, Change the "name" to "cityList".
  11. Go to the "Variables" block and drag the "initialize app variable name to" block. Also, Change the "name" to "temperature".
  12. Go to the "Screen2" and drag "When Screen2 Starts".
  13. Go to the "Variables" and drag "set app variable APIKey to" on the screen.
  14. Now select "app variable cityList" from the drop-down.
  15. Go to the "Lists" and drag the "list" on the screen.
  16. Go to the "Text" and drag the first block on the screen.
  17. Delete the "1" block.
  18. Go to the "Text" and drag the first block on the screen.
  19. Delete the "2" block.
  20. Go to the "Text" and drag the first block on the screen.
  21. Go to the "Text" and drag the first block on the screen.
  22. Delete the "3" block.
  23. Go to the "List_Viewer1" and drag the "set List_Viewer1's text items" block on the screen.
  24. Go to the "Variables" and drag the "app variable cityList" block on the screen.
