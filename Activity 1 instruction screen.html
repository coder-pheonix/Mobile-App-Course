Introduction Screen of "Space Collector Game" app

This app consists of three screens:

  1. The first screen is an introduction screen.
  2. The second screen is the game screen.
  3. The third screen is the score screen.

In this session, we will display the instruction to play the game.

Instruction Screen UI

  1. Click on the "Create New App".
  2. Write "New Project Name: Space Collector Game", "category: Games" and then click "Create".
  3. Drag a "Label" to the screen.
  4. Change the name from "Label" to "Title".
  5. Click on the "Label" and type "Instructions" in the "Text" of the property window.
  6. Change the font size from "18" to "35".
  7. Make the text "Space Collector" bold.
  8. Align the text "Space Collector" to centre.
  9. Drag a "Label" to the screen.
  10. Click on the "Label" and type "Instructions" in the "Text" of the property window.
  11. Align the text "Instructions" to centre.
  12. Drag a "Label" to the screen.
  13. Click on the "Label" and type "Collect all the crystals and reach home without touching the Asteroids and Lasers" in the "Text" of the property window.
  14. Align the text "Collect all the crystals and reach home without touching the Asteroids and Lasers" to center.
  15. Drag a "Button" to the screen.
  16. Change the name from "Button" to "Play".
  17. Change the text "Button" to "Let's Play".
  18. Change the value of X and Y to "85" and "534.5", respectively. Also, change the height and width to "50" and "200", respectively.
  19. Final Screen.
